# Privacy Policy for Property Rental

**Effective Date:** 16 Jun, 2026

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to Property Rental ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## 2. Information We Collect
* **Personal Information:** When you register, we may collect your name, email address, phone number, and profile picture.
* **Property Information:** If you list a property, we collect details about the property, images, pricing, and location.
* **Location Data:** With your permission, we collect your precise or approximate location to show you nearby properties.
* **Usage Data:** We collect information about how you interact with our app, including device information, IP address, and app activity.

## 3. How We Use Your Information
We use your information to:
* Provide, maintain, and improve our services.
* Facilitate bookings and communication between landlords and tenants.
* Send important notices, updates, and promotional content.
* Enhance app security and prevent fraud.

## 4. Sharing Your Information
We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:
* **Other Users:** Limited profile information and property details are visible to facilitate rentals.
* **Service Providers:** Third-party services that help us operate our app (e.g., cloud storage, analytics).
* **Legal Requirements:** If required by law or to protect our rights.

## 5. Data Security
We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

## 6. Data Retention and Deletion (Including Facebook Data Deletion)
We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to provide you with our services. You have the right to request the complete deletion of your data at any time.

**How to Delete Your Account and Data within the App:**
You can permanently delete your account and all associated data directly within the Property Rental app:
1. Open the Property Rental app and log in.
2. Navigate to your Profile/Security&Account page.
3. Select the "Delete Account" option.
4. Confirm your request. Once confirmed, your profile, properties, and all associated personal data will be immediately and permanently removed from our servers.

**Facebook Data Deletion Instructions:**
If you registered for Property Rental using Facebook Login and wish to remove your data and disconnect your Facebook account, you can do so by following these steps:
1. Go to your Facebook Account’s **Settings & Privacy** menu and click **Settings**.
2. Scroll down to the "Permissions" section and click on **Apps and Websites**.
3. Find **Property Rental** in the list of active apps and click **Remove**.
4. To ensure all data on our end is completely purged, please use the in-app "Delete Account" feature described above, or email us at the address below to request a manual deletion.
