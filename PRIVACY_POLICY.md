## 1. Introduction

Welcome to Property Rental. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal data of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## 2. Information We Collect

We may collect information about you in several ways when you use the Property Rental application:

- **Account & Registration Data:** Personal details such as your name, email address, phone number, and profile image when you register for an account.
- **Location Information:** With your permission, we collect precise or approximate geo-location details from your mobile device to show properties near you and enable map search features.
- **User-Generated Content (UGC):** Property listings, descriptions, prices, rental terms, and images uploaded by you, as well as chat messages exchanged with other users in the app.
- **Device Data:** Information about your mobile device, including push notification tokens (FCM tokens) to deliver messages and notifications.

## 3. Use of Your Information

Having accurate information about you permits us to provide you with a smooth, efficient, and customized experience. We may use information collected to:

- Create and manage your account.
- Facilitate communication between users (landlords and tenants).
- Verify property details and ensure platform safety.
- Deliver real-time chat alerts and push notifications.
- Monitor and analyze usage and trends to improve your experience.

## 4. UGC & Moderation Policy

Property Rental hosts user-generated property listings and chat messages. In compliance with Google Play Developer Policies:

- We enforce a zero-tolerance policy for objectionable or abusive content.
- Users can report listings or profiles that violate our guidelines.
- We reserve the right to remove any content and suspend or terminate accounts that upload fraudulent, hateful, or abusive content.

## 5. Account Deletion and Data Retention

You can delete your account and associated personal data at any time via the Security & Account settings within the app. Upon request to terminate your account, we will deactivate or delete your account and information from our active databases.

## 6. Security of Your Information

We use administrative, technical, and physical security measures to help protect your personal information. While we have taken reasonable steps to secure the personal information you provide, please note that no security measures are perfect or impenetrable.
