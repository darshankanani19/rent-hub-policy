# Privacy Policy for Rent Hub

**Effective Date:** 16 Jun, 2026

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to Rent Hub ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## 2. Information We Collect

* **Personal Information:** When you register, we may collect your name, email address, phone number, and profile picture.
* **Property Information:** If you list a property, we collect details about the property, images, pricing, and location.
* **Location Data:** With your permission, we collect your precise or approximate location to show you nearby properties.
* **Usage Data:** We collect information about how you interact with our app, including device information, IP address, and app activity.

## 3. How We Use Your Information
We use your information to:
* Provide, maintain, and improve our services.
* Facilitate bookings and communication between landlords and tenants.
* Send important notices, updates, and promotional content.
* Enhance app security and prevent fraud.

## 4. Sharing Your Information
We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

* **Other Users:** Limited profile information and property details are visible to facilitate rentals.
* **Service Providers:** Third-party services that help us operate our app (e.g., cloud storage, analytics).
* **Legal Requirements:** If required by law or to protect our rights.

## 5. Data Security
We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.
